About

With 2 years of experience in the industry, I have walked runways at Paris Fashion Week and shown in Los Angeles and San Diego, building a presence on some of fashion's most prestigious stages.

From Adobe Photoshop to the Disney x Gap collaboration, my campaign work spans some of the most recognizable names in the world.
